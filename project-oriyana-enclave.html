Oriyana Enclave

Your plot. Your vision. Your legacy.

Project Overview

Premium Township
Prime Lucknow Location

Oriyana Enclave is an upcoming ultra-premium gated township located on the rapidly developing Sultanpur Road, crafted to redefine modern living in Lucknow. Spread across 20 acres of prime land, this landmark development is designed to offer a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, connectivity, and lifestyle within a secure and beautifully planned environment.

Built for families who aspire to live a modern and elevated lifestyle, Oriyana Enclave brings together thoughtfully designed infrastructure and world-class amenities to create a truly complete township experience. Residents will enjoy premium lifestyle facilities including a grand clubhouse, fully equipped gymnasium, swimming pool, landscaped parks, jogging track, temple, grocery stores, salon, basketball court, badminton court, volleyball court, children’s play areas, 24×7 security, and advanced CCTV surveillance — everything thoughtfully planned within one gated community.

With modern infrastructure, premium amenities, excellent connectivity, and a future-ready vision, Oriyana Enclave is not just a township — it is a destination where luxury meets peace, convenience meets connectivity, and every family finds the perfect place to build a brighter future.
