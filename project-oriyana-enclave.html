Project Overview

Premium Plots
Prime Lucknow Location

Oriyana Enclave is a thoughtfully master-planned plotted development nestled in one of Lucknow's prime residential corridors. Offering well-proportioned plots with modern infrastructure, the enclave is designed for those who want to build their dream home exactly the way they envision it.

Every aspect of Oriyana Enclave speaks of quality — from the wide asphalted internal roads and underground utilities to the green open spaces and the secure gated perimeter. This is not just land; it is the canvas for your future.

Backed by the trust and excellence of Rare Bird Infra Pvt. Ltd., Oriyana Enclave promises transparent dealings, careful planning, and long-term value in a city that is growing fast.
