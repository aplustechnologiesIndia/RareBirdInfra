Project Overview

A Community Designed
for the Long Term

Rarebird Greens is an 85-acre premium plotted development located in Devanahalli, Bangalore's rapidly growing northern corridor. Conceived with a master plan that places community living at its core, the township blends architectural thoughtfulness with generous natural green cover.

Every road is wide. Every plot is well-proportioned. Every amenity is designed not as an afterthought, but as an integral pillar of daily life. From the moment you enter through the main gate, Rarebird Greens announces itself differently — quieter, more considered, more humane in its pace.

With direct access to the Bangalore International Airport Road and proximity to major IT parks and educational institutes, Rarebird Greens is as well-connected as it is tranquil. This is land with a future — and a present worth enjoying.
