Project Overview

Luxury Villas
Crafted for Life

Rarebird Villas successfully developed and delivered premium 25×50 (1250 sq.ft.) modern row houses in Lucknow that are now completely sold out — a proud milestone reflecting trust, quality, and customer satisfaction.

These beautifully planned homes were developed near Amity University at some of Lucknow’s most promising residential locations, including Dev Residency, Madhav Green, and Ishanika Estate.

Designed with contemporary architecture, spacious layouts, premium finishes, and modern lifestyle features, Rarebird Villas offered the perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and smart living. Every home was thoughtfully crafted to provide families with a peaceful, secure, and future-ready lifestyle.

The complete sellout of these projects is a strong reflection of Rarebird’s commitment to quality construction, timely delivery, and customer trust.
