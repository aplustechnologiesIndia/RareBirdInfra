Project Overview

Elevated Living,
Grounded in Vision

Rarebird Heights is a 65-acre premium hilltop township positioned at an elevation that offers sweeping views of the Hosur plateau and surrounding Cauvery basin landscape. Located along the Hosur–Bangalore industrial corridor — one of India's fastest growing economic zones — this project is a rare convergence of scenic beauty and investment potential.

The township master plan prioritises wide-open sightlines, minimum ground coverage, and maximum breathing room. Plots are carefully oriented to capture the best natural light and ventilation, while roads follow natural topographic contours rather than forcing a rigid grid upon the land.

Hosur's emergence as a major EV and electronics manufacturing hub, coupled with its proximity to Bangalore (just 40 km away), has made this corridor one of the country's most compelling real estate investment destinations. Rarebird Heights is positioned at its most promising address.
